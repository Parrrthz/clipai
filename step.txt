=== ClipAI - How to Run ===

STEP 1 - Open Terminal (Ctrl + Alt + T)

STEP 2 - Run this command:
cd ~/clipai && source venv/bin/activate && python3 app.py

STEP 3 - Open Browser and go to:
http://127.0.0.1:5000

STEP 4 - Paste YouTube URL and click Generate Clips!

STEP 5 - Wait 5-10 minutes for processing

STEP 6 - Download your clips and post on Instagram/TikTok!

=== Done! ===
